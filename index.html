frequently asked questions

what is nothing2watch?

nothing2watch is a website that is catered towards recommending movies based on a list that you make!

cinema is something that I always hold dear to my heart, but one of the problems with that is having so many options that you can't pick something to watch.

to fix that, I've decided to make nothing2watch!

how does this work?

make a list of up to 5 movies and we'll recommend movies based on that list!

we make the recommendations based on a plethora of factors, such as genre, director, publishing studio, reviews, and more.
